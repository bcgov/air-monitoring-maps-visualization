Pollutant objective

An Air Quality Objective is the provincial acceptable limit for the following pollutant concentrations:

  • PM2.5 (24hr) = 25 µg/m3
  • PM10 (24hr) = 50 µg/m3
  • O3 (8hr) = 63 ppb
  • NO2 = 100 ppb
  • SO2 = 70 ppb
  • TRS = 5 ppb

Pollutant objectives will be highlighted by a red bar on the graph. Read more about the province's Air Quality Objectives and Standards.

Abbreviations

  • PM2.5 = fine particles with a diameter of 2.5 microns (millionths of a metre)
  • PM10 = small particles with a diameter of 10 microns
  • O3 = ozone
  • NO2 = nitrogen dioxide
  • SO2 = sulphur dioxide
  • ppb = parts per billion
  • ppm = parts per million
  • µg/m3 = micrograms per cubic metre
  • N/A = Readings are monitored but are not available at this time.
